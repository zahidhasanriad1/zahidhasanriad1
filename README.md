### 👨🏻‍💻 &nbsp;About Me

I am a **Computer Science researcher**, **prospective PhD applicant**, and **Software Engineer** with a strong interest in building intelligent, scalable, and research-oriented software systems.

My work focuses on the intersection of **Deep Learning**, **Computer Vision**, **Natural Language Processing**, **Generative AI**, **Large Language Models**, **Vision-Language Models**, and **Document Intelligence**.

I am open to collaboration on **AI research**, **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **Computer Vision**, **Dataset Creation**, **Document AI**, **Healthcare AI**, and research-oriented software engineering projects.
